## Detailed description (paste into the long description field)

```
Smart Seller Profit Calculator is the fastest way for online sellers to know exactly how much they actually make on every order — before they pack a single item.

Open Chrome, click the icon, type in your numbers, and get profit, margin %, and break-even price in three seconds. No spreadsheets, no app to install, no account to create, no internet connection required after install.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
THE PROBLEM IT SOLVES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Every online seller eventually runs into the same painful moment: an order looks profitable on paper, then you subtract the courier fee, the ad spend per order, the discount you offered the customer, the product cost — and suddenly you're losing money on a sale you already shipped.

Or the opposite: an order looks razor-thin, but you ship it anyway, and at the end of the week you can't tell which products are actually paying your bills and which are just keeping you busy.

Smart Seller Profit Calculator does this math for you in seconds. Type in your product cost, delivery, ad spend, discount, and selling price → see exactly how much you're making, your profit margin %, and the lowest price you can charge before you start losing money.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
BUILT FOR SELLERS, BY A SELLER
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Whether you sell on Shopify, WooCommerce, Daraz, a Facebook Page, Instagram DMs, or you're closing deals over WhatsApp messages — the math behind every order is the same, and this tool fits all of them.

Especially handy for sellers in Bangladesh: every major courier is baked in with both Inside-Dhaka and Outside-Dhaka pricing. Pick a courier from the dropdown, the delivery cost auto-fills. No more memorizing 12 different rate cards.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
WHAT YOU CAN DO
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

✓ Get profit, margin %, and break-even — instantly
   Type in product cost, delivery, ad cost, discount, and selling price.
   See profit (color-coded green for profit, red for loss), profit margin
   percentage, and the exact selling price you'd need to break even on this
   order. Numbers update live as you type — no "Calculate" button to press.

✓ Target Profit Mode — pricing made obvious
   Don't know what to charge? Switch to Target Profit Mode and tell the
   calculator the profit margin you want (say, 30%). It works backwards
   from your costs and gives you the exact selling price you need to set
   to hit that margin. Perfect for pricing new products or running margin
   experiments.

✓ Save & compare every product
   Save every calculation with a name. Build your full catalog of margins
   in one place. Sort by highest profit, highest margin, name, or date.
   Load any saved product back into the calculator with one click —
   tweak the price, see the new profit, save it again. Duplicate items to
   try variations. Compare your products at a glance.

✓ BDT ↔ USD currency toggle
   Switch between Bangladeshi Taka (৳) and US Dollar ($) at any time.
   Configurable exchange rate in Settings — change it whenever the rate
   moves. All values stay accurate when toggling — no rounding errors,
   no lost precision.

✓ 12 Bangladesh courier presets — ready to use
   Pathao, Steadfast, RedX, Sundarban, Paperfly, eCourier — each with
   Inside-Dhaka and Outside-Dhaka pricing baked in. Edit any preset to
   match your negotiated rates, add couriers we haven't covered, or
   reset everything back to defaults anytime.

✓ Works completely offline
   Once installed, no internet needed. Calculate on a flaky connection,
   in the back of a car, between meetings — it just works.
```

### Privacy policy text

```
Privacy Policy — Smart Seller Profit Calculator

Effective date: 2026-05-04

This Chrome extension ("Smart Seller Profit Calculator") is designed to work entirely on your device. It does not collect, store on remote servers, or share any personal data.

DATA WE STORE LOCALLY
The extension stores the following information in your own browser using the chrome.storage.local API:
  • Your saved product calculations (names, costs, prices, computed profit/margin)
  • Courier presets you have added or edited
  • Your selected display currency (BDT or USD) and exchange rate

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, and the extension developer has no access to it.

DATA WE DO NOT COLLECT
We do not collect personally identifiable information, browsing history, location, financial information, login credentials, contact lists, or any other personal data.

THIRD PARTIES
The extension contains no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and makes no network requests.

PERMISSIONS
The extension requests the "storage" permission, which is used solely to read and write the local data described above.

DELETING YOUR DATA
You can clear all saved products from the Saved tab. You can also remove all extension data by uninstalling the extension or by clearing your browser's extension data.

CONTACT
Questions? Open an issue at:
https://github.com/ShajidHassan/Smart-Seller-Profit-Calculator/issues

CHANGES
If this policy changes, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.
```
